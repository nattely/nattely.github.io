Townhouse from 1877

In the heart of Viborg's historic streets, you will find Nattely Bed & Breakfast. With its unique location, you are guaranteed a room with a beautiful view of either the Gråbrødre Monastery or the charming, cobblestoned Sct. Mogens Gade. Thanks to the central location, as a guest, you are never far from pedestrian zones, shopping opportunities, or the vibrant city life and its associated cafes and restaurants.

At Nattely, you are assured a comfortable stay, and you can choose whether you want a single or double room. If you desire a private bathroom, we can accommodate that too. We can also offer an apartment with 3 double rooms, 2 bathrooms, and a large kitchen.

About us

We are a small bed and breakfast, where there is plenty of coziness and tranquility. We offer good beds, wardrobes, TV, and even tables and chairs in all our rooms.

  • Located in the old town of Viborg, the old capital.
  • Free tea and coffee for Nattely's guests.
  • Free wifi.
  • TV in all rooms
  • The opportunity to prepare your breakfast in the guest kitchen.
  • Smoke-free surroundings.
